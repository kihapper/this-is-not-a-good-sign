THIS IS NOT
A GOOD SIGN

AR poetry project by
J. R. Carpenter & Tomo Kihara


About

In this project we use augmented reality to overlay the user's surroundings with signage posing questions about past and present climatic conditions. Why is the sky so blue? Has it always rained?

These signs call attention to the small changes in the climate already occurring all around us, inviting playful responses.


How to play

  1. With your iPhone go to press Start AR
  2. Pick a sign from the list. Select Place in AR to activate the AR.
  3. Point the camera to the ground for the phone to detect a surface.
  4. Adjust size, angle, and placement using two fingers.
  5. Tap the screen and take pictures or videos.
  6. Post pictures or videos on Instagram or Twitter, tagging us @thisisnotagoodsign_ #notagoodsign
